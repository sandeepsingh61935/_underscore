Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Your privacy matters. _underscore is built with privacy as a core principle. We collect as little data as possible and never sell your information.

What We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide you with our services:

  • Account information: Your email address and display name from your OAuth provider (Google or Apple).
  • Highlight data: The text you highlight, the source URL, and a timestamp ‚Äî only when using Memory or Neural mode.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous session counts and feature usage to improve the product. Never tied to your identity.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your browsing history or the pages you visit
  • The full content of any web page
  • Anything from Focus or Capture mode (100% local)
  • Keystroke data, mouse movements, or screen recordings

End-to-End Encryption

All highlights stored in Memory mode are encrypted on your device before being sent to our servers. We use RSA-2048 encryption, meaning even we cannot read your highlights. Only your device holds the decryption key.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard infrastructure. We use Supabase with row-level security policies to ensure your data is isolated from other users.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Export all your data at any time (Settings ‚Üí Export)
  • Delete all your data permanently (Settings ‚Üí Delete all)
  • Review what we store ‚Äî your Collections page shows everything
